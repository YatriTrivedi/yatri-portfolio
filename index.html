Who I Am

I am Yatri Trivedi, a creative Graphic Designer and UI/UX Designer from Anand, Gujarat, India. I have completed both BCA and MCA from SPU University, which gave me a strong technical foundation alongside my creative journey.

Professionally, I have worked as a Junior Software Developer at Garima System Solution Pvt. Ltd., Vadodara, a Frontend Developer at Cutting Edge, Vadodara, and a UI/UX Designer at Tata STRIVE, Anand. This combination of software, frontend, and design experience helps me create modern visual systems, polished interfaces, and brand experiences that are user-friendly and business-focused.
